Sustainability

Many people choose to use collagen products to help nourish their skin and combat the visible effects of ageing, however applying collagen topically has never been shown to stimulate collagen synthesis nor growth. This is because collagens have molecular weights making them too large to penetrate the top layer of skin.  When supplemented orally with vitamin c for collagen synthesis, you can enjoy an abundance of health and beauty anti-ageing benefits.

Our Collagen
Our Bovine collagen is sourced from materials that are by-products from the already established cattle industries.  Using the cattle hides that were to be discarded helps to reduce the waste and environmental impact within the livestock industry.
Our Packaging
Our eco-friendly bottles are made using RPET, recycled plastic that was headed for landfills to cause more pollution.  This sustainable option is 100% recyclable and uses less energy to both recycle and manufacture than other PET plastic bottles, lowering the carbon footprint.  Its exceptional capacity-to-weight ratio also helps cut fuel costs during transportation, conversely lessening CO2 emissions to the environment.
Our Community
At Glo Collagen Water we want to make sure that as we grow, our contribution to the environment also grows.  That is why we reached out to Trees For Cities, a UK charity that are helping to create a healthier environment both nationally and internationally through their work planting trees and educating our future generations.  Find out about how we intend to play our part and what we can do to contribute to important causes like that of Trees For Cities.
