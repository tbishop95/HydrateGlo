Sustainability

There are many stages involved with creating a bottle of Glo Collagen water, from sourcing 100% natural artesian spring water, to obtaining the raw materials and producing the highest quality collagen peptides. Each stage of this process is scrutinised to ensure our product journey is both sustainable and ethical, so a bottle of Glo Collagen Water meets the highest environmental standards.

Our Collagen
Our natural, high quality bovine collagen is recycled from by-products of the meat industry, reducing waste and the environmental impact within the livestock sector. We use environmental performance indicators within production to continually reduce our ecological footprint, whilst ensuring only healthy grass fed animals, clean air and clear water are the make up of our high quality collagen
Our Packaging
Our eco-friendly bottles are made using RPET, recycled plastic that was headed for landfills and our oceans. This sustainable option is 100% recyclable and uses less energy to both recycle and manufacture than other PET plastic bottles, lowering the carbon footprint. Its exceptional capacity-to-weight ratio also helps cut fuel costs during transportation, minimising CO2 emissions to the environment. We urge our customers to recycle their bottles after use, doing their part to ensure this plastic can be used again and again in this sustainable cycle.
Our Community
At Glo Collagen Water we want to make sure that as we grow, our contribution to the environment also grows. That is why we reached out to Trees For Cities, a UK charity that are helping to create a healthier environment, both nationally and internationally, through their work planting trees and educating our future generations.
