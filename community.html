Our Community

Charities play a vital role in our society, bringing people together who care about a cause so that we can all benefit. We have created a community of charities that are close to our hearts, involved with causes that have formed our identity. We promise to donate 10% of all profits to our chosen charities, so that they can continue to make a difference.

National Autistic Society
One in every hundred people are on the autism spectrum, affecting how people communicate and interact with the world. Autism is a lifelong developmental condition and can affect people in different ways, meaning the support needed for individuals and their families is vastly differing and ever changing. Making society work for autistic people is a highly complex challenge and the work that the National Autistic Society does is vital to hundreds of thousands of lives across the UK.
MS Society
Multiple Sclerosis, or MS is a neurological condition where the immune system which normally protects us, instead attacks your nerves affecting many parts of the body. MS is an extremely complex condition with varying symptoms which at present is incurable. The research carried out by the MS Society is not only crucial to helping us understand the condition so we can better manage the symptoms, but one day we may be able to stop MS all together.
